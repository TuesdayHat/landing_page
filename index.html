  • Games

    More generally than just tabletop; I'm interested in what makes a rule set work, whether they're absolute like in a videogame or more of a suggenstion that all participants agree to follow because it makes the proess more enjoyable. Because that's all games are, at their core -- you've agreed to follow rules when you don't have to for no benefit other than that the act of following those rules is more enjoyble than not doing that.
